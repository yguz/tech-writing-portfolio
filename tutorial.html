  • Tutorial Video/Instruction Set

Collaborating with my group for the instruction set and video assignment was a great experience; we all got along well and communicated effectively to get everything done. The only challenge we faced while working on this assignment was that we had to record the instruction video twice since it had a lag when we first recorded it. From this assignment, I learned that when making an instructional video, it is essential to include a lot of visual aids. Once the instructional video starts, you must provide a brief background to ensure the audience knows what they will be watching.
